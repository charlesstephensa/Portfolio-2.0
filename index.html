Hello, I'm Charles Stephens.

Ever since I was a child I always seemed to gravitate towards technology, It has always been a major part of my life, from playing with consoles at a young age to learning how to bypass the parental controls to get more hours on my PC when my dad wasn't home. Technology and the internet has always been my calling. I wish to push the boundries of what I can do with computers and continue a life of learning new things.

About Me

I spent the developing years of my life traveling the world with my family, living abroad in places like (but not limited to), Tokyo Japan, Nairobi Kenya, Ethiopia, and Berlin, before moving to the United States shortly after middle school.

I spent most of my freetime playing with my friends and exploring emerging markets like Virtual Reality and the Blockchain. If im not learning, coding or chatting with friends I enjoy hiking.
